COMPANY OVERVIEW

Kisanji Enterprises specializes in temperature-controlled road transport, ensuring the safe and timely delivery of perishable goods across Kenya. With a modern fleet of refrigerated trucks, we provide reliable logistics for the food, pharmaceutical, and other temperature-sensitive industries, earning trust through excellence and precision.
